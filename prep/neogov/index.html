Quick context on the role

NEOGOV (govtech SaaS, ~$3B EQT + CPP acquisition July 2025) wants a Revenue AI Architect to own the end-to-end AI Revenue Operating System: data architecture, signal taxonomy, four named output artifacts (CRO brief / Director scorecard / seller action sheet / Marketing Intelligence brief), and the production scaffolding around all of it.

Tech stack: Salesforce + Outreach + Gong + Tableau + 6Sense / Pursuit.us / Starbridge for intent signals. Likely Snowflake or BigQuery warehouse. Likely Clari for forecasting. AI substrate is the architect's choice (custom on OpenAI/Anthropic vs. Snowflake Cortex vs. Salesforce Agentforce — likely hybrid).

Success metric per JD: "changed behaviors and direct pipeline impact, NOT output volume" — this phrase is the gravitational center of the role and shows up in every category of the prep.
