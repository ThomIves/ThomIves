ON Semi's PITAD team — Production Image Test Algorithm Development — built and maintained the computer-vision tests that ran against die imaged while still on the wafer, before packaging. The products were imaging die that went into automotive vision sensors, GoPro-class cameras, and high-end cinematography — all applications where a bad die reaching a customer meant real consequences.

The point was simple: catch bad die before we sold them. We had a continuous improvement loop — feedback from production drove test refinements, which ran through an established test system, which released on cadence. Every product got its own group of tests.
