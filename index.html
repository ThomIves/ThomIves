About

I'm a Full-Stack AI App Architect and Data Scientist with 40+ years across consulting, enterprise R&D, naval nuclear power, and founder-owned ventures. Currently a Senior AI Consultant at Unify Consulting on Microsoft's Account-Based Marketing AI team, building reusable analytical infrastructure on the Microsoft Fabric and Delta lakehouse stack.

Underneath the AI work is a Mechanical Engineering Ph.D. (Texas A&M, 1997) with research focus on multi-physics predictive modeling and controls — hybrid vehicle power plant design methodologies. Sixteen years at HP across Disk Arrays, MEMS R&D, and product engineering produced 26 US Patents in electromechanical devices. Five peer-reviewed publications in IEEE Transactions on Robotics & Automation and the Journal of Imaging Science and Technology. Adjunct teaching at Boise State University (Control Systems Design, five semesters) and the University of Idaho (Design of Experiments).

I run two ventures with end-to-end ownership: PostalDataPI — a global postal-code validation and enrichment API I built agent-native from day one for direct AI-agent consumption — and Fungaia Coffee Company, where I'm Director of Technology and own the full Next.js e-commerce stack. I'm also building JosephCAD as personal research — a browser-native 3D CAD system with AI-agent-assisted drawing.

Earlier I founded Integrated Machine Learning & AI, growing a worldwide mentoring community to 65K+ followers. Idaho has been my professional home since 1986, when I began as a Naval Nuclear Program supervisor at the Naval Reactors Facility in Idaho Falls. I live in Eagle, Idaho.

Currently open to consulting engagements and senior architect / principal roles.

Echo Global Logistics — Outlook AI Add-In for single-shipment quotes (Senior Data Scientist)

Sales reps were receiving 30,000+ single-shipment quote requests per day via email — not managed accounts, so each request consumed sales-rep time for little profit. The software dev team was not available for front-end work, so I developed the full stack: an Outlook Add-In LLM-based AI Web App. Quote-request handling dropped from 5+ minutes to 15 seconds. Meaningful margin recovery for the company and compensation lift for the reps in the field.

ON Semiconductor — autonomous wafer-level vision-test automation (2016)

ON Semiconductor's wafer-level vision test for digital-camera imaging die took 2+ person-weeks per tuning to identify subtle defects other tests missed. After tenured teammates said it couldn't be automated, I built it on my own time in the evenings. Three months later: x/y pixel positions as features, grayscale values as labels, 5th-order feature engineering for a fit that wasn't overfit, statistical-band clustering for pass/fail. The application graded better than I did manually.

Research

Ph.D. Dissertation — Methodologies for the Design and Modeling of Hybrid Vehicle Power Plants

Texas A&M University, August 1997. Multi-physics modeling and design methodologies for hybrid vehicle power plants using bond-graph methods. Modular component models (engines, induction motors, batteries, thermal-fluid subsystems), series / parallel / complex hybrid architecture exploration, and critical-design-parameter identification. Chair: Dr. T. R. Lalk. Download PDF · Read summary & figures

M.S. Thesis — Robot Calibration Without Scaling

Texas A&M University, May 1995. A robot calibration methodology that avoids matrix scaling by restructuring the calibration equations to produce well-conditioned matrices from the start. Validated with simulated data and real-robot measurements. Three peer-reviewed IEEE publications followed. Calibration work also fed NASA experiments for Space Shuttle Robot Arm operations. Advisor: Dr. Louis J. Everett. Download PDF · Read summary
